PRD — Smart Review Queue

Epic ID: CF-604   Author: Product Owner   Status: In Review   Created: Jul 18, 2026

1. Problem Statement

Reviewers waste roughly forty minutes each shoot hopping between three separate tools to approve product images. Approvals slip through the cracks because there is no single, shared queue.

Goal: cut review time in half and give every reviewer one shared queue across all active shoots.

2. Goals

  • Cut review time per shoot by 50%
  • One shared approval queue across every active shoot
  • Keyboard-first navigation through the queue
