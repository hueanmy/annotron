## Tips for Recording

- **Background:** Use your demo video (annotron-demo-v2.mp4) or a screen recording of the app in action
- **B-roll timing:** Sync narration with feature highlights:
  - "Point to any element" → show clicking on elements
  - "feedback automatically applies" → show agent making changes
  - "runs locally" → show localhost URL bar
- **Music:** Add subtle background music (royalty-free, 20-30% volume under voice)
- **Text overlays:** Subtitles or key terms as emphasis
